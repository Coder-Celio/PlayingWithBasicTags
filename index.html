Varations of coffee

Coffee is one of the beverages that many people drink in moring to start off their day. There are 3 very different of roast that coffee have

Light Roast

  • Light brown in color
  • High Acidity
  • fruity Smell

Medium Roast

  • Medium brown in color
  • Balanced flavor/acidity
  • Slightly sweeter in taste

Dark Roast

  • Rich darker color
  • Oily Surface
  • Bitter smoky taste
